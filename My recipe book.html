Lord Krsna's cuisine by HG Yamuna Devi Mataji

The yumiliciud delicious food items that are offered to Lord Sri Krsna via the transparent medium of disciplic
succession through Srila Prabhupada.

Kacohri

Ingridients:

  • Flour
  • Peace paste
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Salt(2 tablespoons)

Procedure:

  1. Take two hands of flour and place that in a plate.
  2. Put some water into that plate and knead that mixture firmly.
  3. Make the dough carefully and take small peaces from that dough and roll it.
  4. Put the mixture of peace amidst of that dough and again roll it.
  5. Then fry it and offer it to Krsna.
